Bloody Mary

Taken from here

Description

A yummy bloody mary

You really can't mess these up

Ingredients

  • 4 cups tomato juice
  • 1 1/3 cups vodka
  • 3 lemons, juiced
  • 1 1/2 tbps worcestershire sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbps horseradish
  • 3/4 tsp hot sauce
  • 1/2 tsp celery salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Instructions

  1. Stir everything together
  2. Put in a pitcher, serve individually
  3. Garnish with celery, bacon, and olives
